PixMob DJ Keyboard

How to use:

  1. Connect your board: Click "Connect to board" and select your Arduino/ESP32.
  2. Map keys: Switch to "Mapping Mode", click a key on the keyboard, select an effect, and save.
  3. Trigger effects: Switch to "Trigger Mode" and press keys (keyboard or click) to send IR commands!
  4. Save/Load: Your mappings are automatically saved. Use Export/Import to backup or share.

Tips:

  • Tail codes add fade effects to base colors
  • Physical keyboard and on-screen clicks both work
  • Mappings persist between sessions
  • Use number keys for quick color access
